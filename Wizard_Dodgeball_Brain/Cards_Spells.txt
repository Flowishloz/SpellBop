SPELL & CARD ARCHITECTURE SPECIFICATION: WIZARD DODGEBALL

===============================================================================
1. CORE DATA TEMPLATE (GLOBAL PROPERTIES)
===============================================================================
Every single card in the 51-card pool inherits from this base data class 
(implemented as a Godot Custom Resource).

- spell_name (String): Unique identifier.
- faction (Enum): RED (Adrenaline), BLUE (Control), GREEN (Momentum).
- rarity (Enum): COMMON, UNCOMMON, RARE.
- cast_time (Float): The duration (in seconds) the player must hold the input 
  to charge and cast the spell. Generates mana sequentially during the hold.
- is_reactive_only (Bool): Determines if the card can only be played onto the stack.
- ui_sprite (Texture2D): The 2D card art face displayed during the draft phase.
- world_sprite (Texture2D): Pixel art sprite used for the projectile/wall in 3D space.

===============================================================================
2. CATEGORY ARCHITECTURES
===============================================================================

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY A: ATTACK (PROJECTILES) - PROACTIVE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Spells designed to cross the arena floor on the Z-axis to inflict damage.

Properties:
- base_speed (Float): Travel velocity across the court.
- projectile_size (Float): Multiplier for the 3D collider and sprite scaling.
- damage (Int): Numeric value applied to opponent's health pool on hit.
- bounciness (Int): Number of wall ricochets allowed before self-destruction.
- lifetime (Float): Maximum duration in seconds before the projectile expires.
- projectile_count (Int): Number of projectiles spawned simultaneously (e.g., spread-shot).

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY B: DEFENSE & UTILITY - PROACTIVE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Static or dynamic barriers and mobility modifiers deployed in real-time neutral play.

Properties:
- wall_size (Vector2): The physical width and height of the barrier collider.
- wall_lifetime (Float): How long the physical shield remains active in the arena.
- movement_speed (Float): Lateral (X-axis) speed of the wall if it moves.
- is_exploding (Bool): If true, detonates on impact, disrupting nearby projectile physics.
- rebound_window (Float): Frame window for a "Perfect Parry" to trigger hyper-speed returns.
- healing_absorb (Bool): Converts incoming projectile damage straight into player health.

*SPECIAL SUB-TYPE: CHARGING-SYNERGY UTILITY*
Designed for heavy high-mana value decks to mitigate the risk of long cast times.
- cast_while_charging (Bool): If true, this card can be cast for FREE without breaking 
  your current mana-charging channel for another spell.
- movement_dash_distance (Float): Instantly surges the player left/right along the baseline.
- conditional_draw (Int): Number of cards drawn instantly upon successful execution.
- Example Use-Case: Cast a heavy Green spell -> hold down button (slowed movement) -> 
  opponent fires an attack -> fire this card to instantly dash away safely and draw a card 
  without dropping your heavy spell charge.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY C: COUNTER (REFLEX & PHASE) - REACTIVE ONLY
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cards locked during neutral play, accessible ONLY during the Time-Slow Stack Window.

Properties:
- opportunity_window (Float): Active frames allowed to resolve the counter spell.
- teleport_distance (Float): Instantly offsets player X-axis position to clear an lane.
- freeze_duration (Float): Temporarily stops opponent movement or tracking mid-match.
- speed_modifier (Float): Flat velocity multiplier applied to redirected projectiles.
- phase_immunity (Float): Inflicts brief, total iframe duration to phase through damage.

===============================================================================
3. BALANCING BASELINE: THE BASE FIREBALL
===============================================================================
Every player possesses an infinite-use Base Fireball that does not occupy a card slot.

- Damage: 1 Unit (Health resets each round; 6 Base Fireball hits = Round KO).
- Speed: Medium.
- Size: Small.
- Cast Time: Minimal/Standard.
- Interaction: 2 Uncommon Large Fireballs (3 Units Damage each) = Round KO.

===============================================================================
4. STATE MACHINE & STACK RESOLUTION
===============================================================================

NEUTRAL STATE (Real-Time 100% Speed)
- Players move left/right on baseline.
- Can cast Attack and Proactive Defense/Utility cards.
- Counter cards are locked/greyed out.

THE STACK STATE (Triggered on Attack/Defense Cast)
- Global engine time slows down drastically to 10% speed.
- Cast card displays clearly along the top of the screen.
- Target player's Counter cards instantly unlock.
- Opportunity window counts down. If Counter is cast, execute reactive logic. 
  If window expires, resume 100% real-time speed for physical dodging/wall parrying.

===============================================================================
5. POOL DISTRIBUTIONS & FACTION THEMES
===============================================================================
Total Launch Pool: 51 Cards (17 per Faction) | Deck Size: 15 Cards.

- COMMONS (39 Total, 13 per Faction): Generic, readable, clean standard visual animations.
- UNCOMMONS (9 Total, 3 per Faction): Low-effort, minor bespoke character animations.
- RARES (3 Total, 1 per Faction): High-effort, screen-shaking, fully custom mechanical logic.

FACTION PATHS:
- RED (Adrenaline): Excels in high speed, fast cast times, and "Near Miss" synergy modifiers.
- BLUE (Control): Focuses heavily on Reactive Counters, freeze states, and projectile trajectory manipulation.
- GREEN (Momentum): Slow speeds, massive health/colliders, healing absorption, and charging-synergy dashes.

===============================================================================
6. MOBILE UI & INPUT ERGONOMICS (THE CASTING BUFFER)
===============================================================================
Optimized explicitly for a dual-thumb mobile control layout (Left Thumb = Character Movement, Right Thumb = Card Selection & Casting).

- Screen Layout: During active gameplay rounds, the player's hand of cards is docked vertically along the right side of the screen.
- Card Minimal State: To prevent screen clutter, cards in the round UI are partially hidden/collapsed. They display only two critical pieces of information: their Spell Type (Attack, Defense, Counter) and their casting cost represented as a small 1, 2, or 3 segment gauge bar.
- The Mid-Cast Buffer (0.5 to 1.0 Second Grace Period):
  * When a player lifts their right thumb off a card mid-charge, the engine does NOT instantly cancel the spell or erase their gathered mana progress.
  * The game freezes and caches the current casting state and mana progress for a strict 0.5 to 1.0 second window.
  * Function A (Ergonomic Execution): This allows the player to lift their thumb off a heavy casting spell, instantly tap an adjacent "Charging-Synergy Utility" card (such as a free evasive dash) to dodge danger, and not drop their primary spell's channel.
  * Function B (Accidental Release Protection): Protects players from touch-screen friction or sweaty thumbs. If contact is accidentally broken, they can resume casting seamlessly by re-pressing the card before the buffer window expires.
