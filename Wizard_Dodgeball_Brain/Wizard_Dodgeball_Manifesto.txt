GAME MANIFESTO: WIZARD DODGEBALL (WORKING TITLE)

1. CORE PHILOSOPHY
- Fast, 3-to-5 round matches blending twitch-reflex physics with strategic deckbuilding.
- A game of reflexes, dexterity, and strategy.
- Deep tactical choices via a 10-second breather between rounds for hand management.
- Zero "dead states": A baseline standard fireball ensures players are always active, even with an empty hand.

2. COMBAT & PHYSICS SYSTEM
- Plane: 2D movement (Left/Right) focusing on positioning and timing.
- Base Mechanics: Cast spells using mana generated by holding a card (slows movement, adds risk). 
- The Stack: Playing a card triggers a time-slow window. The card is telegraphed at the top of the screen, allowing opponents to react with an 'instant' or counter-spell.
  * [SUPERSEDED — Sprint 23 batch 3, 2026-06-16, Creative Director] The Stack window NO LONGER slows
    time — it is now a NORMAL-SPEED telegraph + real-time countdown (the card is still telegraphed at
    the top and opponents still react with an 'instant'/counter during it; only the 10% "bullet time"
    was removed). Slow-motion is now RESERVED for IMPACT moments — a shield reflect, a player taking
    damage (a brief crunch easing into a ~2 s real-time slow-mo), and the death/KO beat. The original
    "playing a card = bullet time" framing below (§2's defining-mechanic intent) is preserved as the
    historical design; the live behaviour is in core/the_stack.gd (the_stack.stack_time_scale export is
    now VESTIGIAL) and HANDOFF.md / Project_State.txt's Sprint 23 entries. Rationale: the constant
    every-card slow-mo had become the default rather than a punctuation; reserving slow-mo for impacts
    makes each one read as an EXPLOSIVE beat (the CD's "make every action explosive" phase).
- Health & Damage: Health resets completely each round. 
  * Base Fireball: 6 hits to kill (medium speed, small).
  * Large Fireball (Uncommon): 2 hits to kill (large, fast).
- Defense & Parries: 
  * Counter Spell: Redirects projectile at 2x speed.
  * Defensive Wall: Physical collider parry. Redirect speed scales with the proximity of the wall to the projectile at cast time (perfect parries create hyper-speed returns).
  * Terminal Velocity: Capped max speed to maintain physics stability and readable gameplay.

3. ECONOMY & MONETIZATION
- Free-to-play model inspired by MTG Arena.
- Level-up rewards include card packs.
- Premium currency strictly reserved for cosmetic card variants, player skins, and bespoke animations. No pay-to-win mechanics.

4. CARDS & DECKBUILDING
- Total Launch Pool: 51 Cards (17 per faction).
- Deck Size: 15 Cards.
- Draft Mechanic: During the 10-second break, players choose to either DRAW a card OR gain an OFF-COLOR MANA token to support multi-faction decks.
- Rarity Economy: 
  * Commons (39 total): Generic, clean visual animations to maintain readable screen state.
  * Uncommons (9 total, 3 per color): Small, low-effort bespoke animations.
  * Rares (3 total, 1 per color): High-effort, game-stopping bespoke animations. 

5. FACTIONS & ELEMENTS
- RED (Adrenaline): High speed, fast cast times, lower health. Focuses on "Near Miss" synergies and high-risk/high-reward pressure.
- BLUE (Control): Average speed/health, highly efficient mana. Focuses on phasing, walls, and manipulating projectile physics/direction.
- GREEN (Momentum): Slow movement, long cast times, high health. Focuses on scaling power from missing health and area-of-effect hazards.
